How to use Piso Wifi

  • Step 1: Open your wifi.
  • Step 2: Connect to Bujoy Piso Wifi.
  • Step 3: Click Sign Up for Network.
  • Step 3.1: If the Sign Up for Network did not pop up, go to your phone's browser.
  • Step 3.2: Type "10.0.0.1".
  • Step 3.3: You will reach this page.
  • Step 4: Insert your coin to the Piso Wifi Machine
  • Step 5: You must insert your coin before the time stops.
  • Step 6: Enjoy your Internet!
